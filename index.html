Coaching Role

Currently the U18 Goalkeeper Coach in South Korea, Faïçal focuses on technical skill development, tactical understanding, and mental resilience for young athletes preparing for high-level competition.

  • Develop technically sound, confident goalkeepers
  • Focus on mental strength and decision-making under pressure
  • Build agility, positioning, and ball distribution skills
  • Prepare young athletes for professional-level competition
